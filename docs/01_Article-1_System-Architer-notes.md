Option 1: The Systems Engineering & Architecture Angle
This topic focuses purely on the IT/OT convergence and how you handle the extreme diversity of data (from fast electrical to slow biological) using a unified digital pipeline.

Working Title: Architecting the Digital RES Learning Factory: Validation of a Scalable MING+React Telemetry Pipeline for Multi-Vector Energy Systems

Core Research Question: How can a Canonical Data Model and edge-to-cloud middleware architecture (MING+React) be structured to normalize and pre-validate highly heterogeneous energy data (electrical vs. biological) prior to physical sensor integration?

Required Data Streams: Simulated JSON superset payloads comparing high-frequency Engine Bench dynamics (RPM, torque, exhaust temperature) against slow-moving Algae Photobioreactor dynamics (pH, dissolved oxygen, optical density).

Target Journal/Conference Profile: IEEE Internet of Things Journal or IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics.
