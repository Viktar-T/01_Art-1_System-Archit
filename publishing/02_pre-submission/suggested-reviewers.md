## What MDPI expects

- 3–5 suggested reviewers (more is fine).
- Each with: full name, current affiliation, institutional email, ORCID or scholar link, 1–2 sentence rationale.
- No conflicts: not a co-author in the last 3–5 years, not at the same institution, no PhD-supervisor relationship, no current collaboration/grant.

## How this list was assembled

1. Searched MDPI *Energies*, *Electronics*, *Sensors* (2023–2025) for Polish first/senior authors whose work overlaps with: MQTT-based energy telemetry, IIoT data architectures for RES/microgrids, open-source SCADA replacements, ML on building/grid automation data.
2. Verified each candidate's institutional affiliation on the university's own page.
3. Cross-checked that none are co-authors of Viktar Taustyka or Kelvyn George Melcheizedek Kanchipogu and none are at the KIOZE / Edu-Bad host institution.
