## Possible discounts & waivers

Discounts that historically have applied to MDPI submissions — check eligibility BEFORE submitting (the APC is calculated against the manuscript record):

- **Institutional Open Access Program (IOAP)** — does the corresponding author's institution participate? https://www.mdpi.com/ioap
- **Reviewer / Editor / Guest Editor vouchers.**
- **Special Issue invited submission** — sometimes carries a discount; check the SI page.
- **Country-based discount** (varies; rarely substantial for European institutions).

## Our funding plan

- **Funding source for APC:** **Faculty of Environmental Management and Agriculture (Wydział Kształtowania Środowiska i Rolnictwa, WKSiR), ZUT Szczecin** — under the Faculty's standing internal regulation that covers APCs for articles published in journals scored **≥ 140 punktów** on the Polish MEiN (Ministry of Education and Science) journal list.
- **Eligibility rationale:** *Energies* is listed at **140 punktów MEiN**, meeting the threshold. The entitlement is automatic under the regulation; no per-article approval letter from the Dean's office is required.
- **Crossref Funder Registry name:** *Not applicable* — the APC payer is an internal university faculty, not a registered grant funder. Do not enter a Funder ID for the APC in the MDPI portal beyond naming the Faculty/University.
- **Backup plan if primary funding unavailable:** Re-verify *Energies* MEiN score immediately before submission. If the score drops below 140 pkt in a revision, escalate to KIOZE / PI to identify alternative APC funding (ZUT central pool, a grant line, or IOAP discount) before submitting.
- **Decision-maker for purchase order:** WKSiR Dean's office (Dziekanat WKSiR) — invoice processed via the standard institutional channel against the faculty's APC budget line.
